# Mouse: P.I. For Hire - The Ultimate Weapon Upgrade Guide

## 1. The Golden Rule: Don't Miss Out!
In *Mouse: P.I. For Hire*, the city of Mouseburg is a dangerous place, and your guns are your best friends. But here is the most important thing you need to know: **The game is linear, and there is no "Level Select."**

If you miss a Blueprint (the items used to upgrade guns) in a mission and move on to the next one, you can never go back to get it. This makes every Blueprint very valuable!

## 2. How Upgrading Works (The 1-3-5 Rule)
Every gun has three levels of power. To upgrade them, you need to spend Blueprints at the workbench in your office. The cost increases each time:

* **Tier 1:** Costs 1 Blueprint
* **Tier 2:** Costs 3 Blueprints
* **Tier 3 (Max):** Costs 5 Blueprints

To fully max out one gun, you need a total of **9 Blueprints**.

## 3. Doing the Math: The Big Achievement
There is a special achievement called **"We'll B.A.N.G., ok!"** which requires you to fully upgrade 8 different weapons. Here is the math for that:

**8 weapons x 9 Blueprints = 72 Blueprints needed.**

There are **89 Blueprints** in the whole game. This means you can afford to miss 17 Blueprints and still get the achievement. It sounds like a lot, but they are very easy to miss!

### Blueprint Locations
| Source | Amount | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Found in Levels | 81 | Hidden in safes, vents, and behind breakable walls. |
| Side Missions | 8 | Rewards for helping NPCs (like the "Poster Girl" mission). |
| **Total** | **89** | **The absolute maximum you can find.** |

## 4. Top 3 Weapons to Upgrade First
Not all guns are created equal. Here are the ones you should focus on:

* **James Gun (Thompson):** This is your all-around best friend. It has a high fire rate and plenty of ammo. Upgrading it to Tier 3 makes it super steady with almost no recoil.
* **Boomstick (Shotgun):** Perfect for close-range combat. Level it up to get "Piercing Shots" that can hit multiple enemies at once.
* **Micer Pistol:** Don't ignore your starting gun! At Tier 3, it gets an "Auto-Tracking" mode that helps you hit targets from far away.

## 5. Don't Forget the Boss Loot!
Even if you have enough Blueprints, you can't reach Tier 3 (the max level) without special items dropped by Bosses. For example:

* **Crocodile Scales:** Dropped by Vic Gatto. Needed to max out the James Gun.
* **Ghost Essence:** Needed to give your Pistol the auto-tracking ability.

## 6. Quick Safety Checklist
1.  **Save Often:** The game gives you 50 save slots. Use them! Before finishing a big mission, save in a new slot so you can go back if you realize you missed a Blueprint.
2.  **Check Every Corner:** If you see a wooden pallet or a cracked wall, break it. Blueprints are often hidden behind them.
3.  **Talk to Everyone:** Some side missions give you up to 4 Blueprints just for choosing the right dialogue!
